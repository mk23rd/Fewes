Frequently Asked Questions

How does the meal kit delivery work?

+

We deliver fresh meal kits to your doorstep every week. Each kit includes pre-portioned ingredients and easy-to-follow recipe cards. Simply choose your plan, select your meals, and we'll handle the rest!

Can I customize my meal selections?

+

Yes! You can choose from our wide variety of vegetarian, meat, and pescatarian options each week. Select only the meals you want to try.

What if I need to skip a week?

+

No problem! You can easily skip weeks, pause, or cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. We're completely flexible.

How long do the ingredients stay fresh?

+

Our meal kits are designed to stay fresh for up to 5 days when stored properly in your refrigerator. We use insulated packaging and ice packs to ensure freshness during delivery.
